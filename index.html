About Me

I am a passionate Aerospace Engineer with expertise in mechanical design, prototype development, and aerospace dynamics. Currently pursuing a PhD in Aerospace Engineering at RMIT University, expected to complete in December 2024.

My work focuses on innovative solutions in aerospace systems, including controlling the Dzhanibekov effect and developing cutting-edge prototypes.
